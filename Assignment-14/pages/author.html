About the Author

Neil deGrasse Tyson is an American astrophysicist, author, and science communicator. He is known for his ability to popularize science and make complex topics accessible to the general public. One of his notable works is the book titled "Astrophysics for People in a Hurry."

"Astrophysics for People in a Hurry" is a concise introduction to astrophysics that explores the mysteries of the universe in a digestible format. In this book, Tyson covers a wide range of topics including the origins of the universe, the nature of dark matter and dark energy, black holes, and the search for extraterrestrial life.

Whether you're a seasoned science enthusiast or someone looking to expand your knowledge of the cosmos, "Astrophysics for People in a Hurry" offers an engaging and informative journey through the wonders of the universe.
