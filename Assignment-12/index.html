Welcome to our Tire Changing Tutorial!

Changing a tire is a necessary skill for every driver. Whether you're on the road or in your driveway, knowing how to change a tire can save you time and money. Follow our step-by-step guide below to learn how:

  1. Park your vehicle on a flat, stable surface and engage the parking brake.
  2. Locate the spare tire, jack, and lug wrench in your vehicle.
  3. Loosen the lug nuts on the flat tire, but do not remove them completely.
  4. Position the jack under the vehicle's frame near the flat tire and raise the vehicle until the flat tire is off the ground.
  5. Remove the lug nuts and then the flat tire.
  6. Mount the spare tire onto the wheel studs and tighten the lug nuts by hand.
  7. Lower the vehicle to the ground using the jack and then tighten the lug nuts securely with the lug wrench.
  8. Double-check all lug nuts to ensure they are tightened properly.
  9. Stow away your tools and flat tire, and you're ready to hit the road again!
