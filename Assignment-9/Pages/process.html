Baking Process for Basic Bread

Follow these simple steps to bake your own delicious bread:

  1. Mix Ingredients: Combine bread flour, water, yeast, and salt in a mixing bowl.
  2. Knead the Dough: Knead the dough on a floured surface until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  3. Let the Dough Rise: Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a kitchen towel, and let it rise until doubled in size.
  4. Shape the Dough: Punch down the dough to release air bubbles, then shape it into a loaf or desired shape.
  5. Bake the Bread: Preheat the oven, place the shaped dough onto a baking sheet or in a bread pan, and bake until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Cool and Enjoy: Allow the bread to cool on a wire rack before slicing and enjoying!
