Ingredients for Basic Bread

Get ready to bake with these essential ingredients:

  • Bread Flour: High-protein flour specially formulated for making bread. It provides structure and strength to the dough.
  • Water: Filtered water at room temperature is ideal for hydrating the dough.
  • Yeast: Active dry yeast or instant yeast is needed for leavening the bread. It helps the dough rise and develop flavor.
  • Salt: Enhances the flavor of the bread and regulates fermentation. It also strengthens gluten formation.

Additional Ingredients (Optional)

These ingredients can add flavor and texture variations to your bread:

  • Sugar: Adds sweetness and helps with yeast fermentation.
  • Olive Oil: Adds richness and moisture to the bread.
  • Milk: Adds tenderness and richness to the crumb.
  • Herbs and Spices: Adds flavor and aroma to the bread.
