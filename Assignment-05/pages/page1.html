Tom Clancy

Thomas Leo Clancy Jr.was an American novelist. He is known best for his ultra-detail writing style which is mainly used to describe Espionage and Military-Science storylines set in and after the Cold-War. He has sold more than 100 million copies of his works worldwide with his writing being used as the imflunce for multiple movies and video games. Tom was a major community member in his hometown of Baltimore and even was the co-owner of the Baltimore Orioles untill his death in 2013.
